Best Practice - Do’s and Dont's

Do’s Dont's
  • Maintain a pipeline with five times your quota to ensure success.
  • Wait to follow up on the lead as they may also be contacting your competitors.
  • Ask Partners (e.g., Accenture) for referrals.
  • Respond to leads promptly, gather information during initial interactions, and determine lead potential.
  • Reach out timely from when the lead is provided – time is of the essence with warm leads.
  • Read the BANT form as attached to the opportunity.
  • Discuss with the SDR on information in the BANT form that you have questions about.
  • Use Salesforce for lead management & KEEP IT UP TO DATE.

Exit Criteria

  • Understanding of initial business need.
  • Meeting set to qualify opportunity.

Best Practice - Do’s and Dont's

Do’s Dont's
  • Understand who to bring into process early for involvement as a customer executive sponsor – Aim High > VP or higher.
  • Sell products into industries that we do not have references that can support your value story.
  • Have customer prove to you they can support indicative pricing models for desired solutions. Our target market is (> $1b).
  • Waste time on low-potential leads or pressure leads into moving forward if they're not ready.
  • Provide RFx Template that aligns to customer requirements to e2open’s differentiation strengths. Customize RFx cover letter (mandate).
  • If communication has slowed or is non-existent, assume competitors have already made inroads.
  • If special products are identified, opportunity ASD will engage peers (e.g., Parcel, LaaS)
  • Waste time with blind RFxs.

Exit Criteria

  • 3 of the 4 BANT criteria are known.
  • Sales Discovery complete and understanding of customer scaling factors are known (spend, inventory, suppliers, modes, trade agreements, etc.).
  • ASD begins initial population of Opportunity Scoping Document (initial solution, business goals, operational challenges) etc.
  • NDA Initiated.

Best Practice - Do’s and Dont's

Do’s Dont's
  • Ask open-ended questions during discovery, gather information on organizational structure, and confirm evaluation plan.
  • Skip discovery, make assumptions about the prospect's needs, or neglect to involve key stakeholders.
  • Do Demos without discovery, without knowing the pain: the purpose of discovery is “figuring out how to win”… do not jump in without taking the time to figure out how to win.

Exit Criteria

  • Discovery meetings completed.
  • Clear view on the customer pain points and how we can bring value and ROI).
  • Org Chart & Power Base chart developed.
  • Evaluation plan in place and collaborative.

Best Practice - Do’s and Dont's

Do’s Dont's
  • Personalize demos, quantify the value of your solution, address objections or concerns.
  • Ignore procurement.
  • Communicate the value of the solution in terms of cost savings, improved efficiency, reduce risk of change management or other tangible benefits.
  • Offer Proof of Concepts (POCs) or pilots.
  • Be prepared to answer technical questions and address customer concerns during demonstrations. Do not be vague.
  • Get Reference Calls, Show Testimonials.

Exit Criteria

  • PSE.
  • Opportunity Approval.
  • Case created for contracts creation.
  • Customer procurement engaged.
  • Validated project is funded and approved.
  • Triangulation Validated (CFO, CIO and CSO).
  • Formal proposal delivered.

Stage Goal

  • Finalize the deal, capturing a fair share of the value.

Best Practice - Do’s and Dont's

Do’s Dont's
  • Know when you’re negotiating.
  • Get out over your skis without management cover.
  • Let them make a counter first, Don’t negotiate with yourself.
  • Use their paper.
  • Test for Procurement “pound of flesh”.
  • Negotiate contract T&C + Price together.
  • Collect all objections before solving any.
  • Track Give/Get.
  • Ask “why”, get to interests not objections.
  • Communicate the value of the solution in terms of cost savings, improved efficiency, or other tangible benefits.
  • Get Reference Calls, Show Testimonials.

Exit Criteria

  • PSE.
  • Infosec/GDPR review complete.
  • Commercials are signed.
  • Final Genus approved.
  • Hub activation.
