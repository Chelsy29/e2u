00-10 Sales Acceptance Lead

GOALS

  • Get a meeting.
  • Determine if there is sufficient reason to believe the lead has potential and should be pursued by Sales.

00-10 Sales Acceptance Lead

QUALIFICATION

  • Spoke with the target, agreed on next steps.
  • There is a reasonable expectation that the opportunity will close within the rolling 12 months.

00-10 Sales Acceptance Lead

DISQUALIFICATION

  • Prospect doesn't have a need for the solution, lacks budget or authority, or is unresponsive. Move to LTD or disqualify.

30-Discovery

GOALS

  • Figure out how to win and to develop a “Coach”.
  • Discover customer pain points, business objectives, and organizational structure to frame the solution’s value proposition.

30-Discovery

QUALIFICATION

  • Discovery meetings complete
  • Next steps scheduled.
  • Org chart & power base chart developed
  • Decision-maker known.
  • Evaluation plan confirmed (verbally or in writing).

30-Discovery

DISQUALIFCATION

  • Prospect doesn't confirm their pain points, doesn't engage key stakeholders, or doesn't commit to the evaluation plan.

40-Teach/Prove

GOALS

  • Prove our value is best.
  • Convince the customer that the solution can address their pain points and provide superior capabilities.

40-Teach/Prove

QUALIFICATION

  • Close Plan agreed upon by Sponsor, E2open selected or shortlisted to two, pricing submitted for approval, and formal proposal or term sheet provided with the customer negotiating back.

40-Teach/Prove

DISQUALIFCATION

  • Prospect doesn't see the solution’s value, doesn't agree to the pricing, or doesn't engage in contract negotiations.

50-Negotiation

GOALS

  • Finalise the deal, Capturing a fair share of the value.
  • Negotiate business and legal terms, including Press Releases and Case Studies.

50-Negotiation

QUALIFICATION

  • MSA, OF, SLA, SOW signed.
  • Involvement of all necessary Stakeholders in the commitment to the Close Plan.

50-Negotiation

DISQUALIFCATION

  • Failure to reach an agreement on business and legal terms or lack of stakeholder commitment to the Close Plan.
